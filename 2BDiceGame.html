THROW_A_DICE

In class, we created a simple dice game and played it on paper. We first established a rule, then rolled the dice to draw elements on the paper according to that rule. We were using two dice. The first dice is six-sided and the second dice is twenty-sided.

THROW_A_DICE_p5

After the class, I created a p5.js version of the dice game. The rule was simplified to make it easier to play. The rule: If the total number of the two dice is odd, draw a circle; if the total number is even, draw a square; if the total is larger than 20, draw a flower.
